About Me

Hi there! I'm Remi, currently a student at the University of Edinburgh, where I'm working on my MEng in Electronics and Computer Science. I grew up in Aberdeen, where my love for the outdoors first began. Whether it was hiking, mountain biking, or just exploring the beautiful landscapes around me with friends. I always found myself drawn to adventure.

Now that I'm in Edinburgh, I'm diving deep into the world of electronics and computer science, figuring out how technology can make life better. I had a great time last summer during my internship at SaxaVord UK Space Port, where I got to see firsthand what goes into operating a spaceport and work on some pretty cool projects.

Lately, I've been focused on developing a range of tech projects that combine my skills in electronics and programming. From creating innovative hardware solutions to working developing my skills creating software, I love tackling challenging problems and bringing ideas to life.

I'm currently on a mission to visit every country in Europe. I love exploring new places and experiencing all the different cultures and landscapes along the way, which inspired the design I chose for this website.
